PVC Films

Products used for PVC Films

    • METHYL TIN STABILIZER
    • EPOXIDIZED SOYBEAN OIL
    • EPOXY PLASTICIZERS
    • PLASTICIZER
    • CALCIUM ZINC STABILIZER

PVC Film also known as Rigid vinyl, is a strong and lightweight film. It is one of the most durable low-cost films and is resistant to many chemicals. It has higher tensile strength and modulus than flexible PVC, but has low impact toughness, and is subject to stress cracking depending on the environment.

PVC film is used as shrink and stretch wrap for industrial and consumer goods and as pallet wrap. Other uses include bags, liners, bottle sleeving, adhesive tape backing, labels, blood bags and I.V. bags. Some other types of PVC Films are Calendaring Film, Shrink Film, Soft Film which are used in various sectors for their features like heat stability, non-toxic food grade packaging material, transparency etc.

The Chemicals manufactured at Shital, are used for as a bending agent that gives the required flexibility to the PVC films.

PVC leather fabric is very similar to PU leather fabric. Instead of polyurethane, PVC leather fabric is made by combining polyvinylchloride with stabilizers (to protect), plasticizers (to soften) and lubricants (to make flexible), and then applying to a base material.

A hose is a flexible tube use for conveying fluids. PVC Hose Pipes or Garden Pipes have great flexible, leak-proof construction, excellent pressure resistance, and longer performance life. These pipes are mainly used for Water Supply and Oil distribution purpose in Industrial, Domestic and Irrigation purpose.
